Random facts about cats

  1. According to Wilde, a slow blink is a “kitty kiss.” This movement shows contentment and trust.
  2. A cat with a question-mark-shaped tail is asking, “Want to play?”
  3. Cats find it threatening when you make direct eye contact with them.
  4. When cats hit you with retracted claws, they're playing, not attacking.
  5. If you can't find your cat, you should look in a box or a bag, as these are some of their favorite hiding spots!
